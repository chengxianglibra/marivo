title: Evidence
description: Findings, propositions, and assessments that make analytical conclusions auditable.
---

Every analysis operator records evidence into the session. Evidence lets an agent explain not only what changed, but how it reached that conclusion.

The evidence store tracks findings, propositions, and assessments. `session.knowledge()` summarizes established facts, driver facts, open anomalies, and suggested follow-ups.

Human reviewers can inspect traces from a proposition back to the findings and assessments that support it.
